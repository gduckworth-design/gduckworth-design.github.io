Major Project HAZOP Workshops Facilitated include:

Project Involvement Description>
BCC Luggage Point Methane Flare HAZOP HAZOP Facilitator Facilitated a HAZOP study for the installation of a biogas flare system for the safe disposal/flaring of digester methane biogas gas at the Brisbane City Council, Luggage Point sewage treatment plant, Brisbane.
Synergen Cyanide Skid HAZOP HAZOP Facilitator Facilitated a HAZOP study for the design and fabrication of a prototype skid mounted, 100 kW thermal plasma based production unit making liquid sodium cyanide from methane or LPG and nitrogen gas for Synergen Met Pty Ltd.
Fekola Gold Project HAZOP HAZOP Facilitator Facilitated a series of HAZOP studies for Lycopodium Minerals Engineering's B2 Gold's 4 Mtpa Mali, Fekola Project covering the main process facility, and subsequently vendor HAZOPs covering the key vendor process equipment including acid wash, elution, electrowinning and carbon regeneration.
Masbate Gold 8 Mtpa Upgrade HAZOP HAZOP Facilitator Facilitated a HAZOP study for the installation of a 3rd Ball mill for Lycopodium Minerals Engineering's B2 Gold project, Masbate Philippines to raise ore processing rate to 8 Mtpa. The existing plant was excluded from the study, and those sections of the plant that involved simple replacement of larger equipment items (e.g. conveyor drive and chute upgrades, and pebble crusher replacement, etc.), were considered in OVERVIEW format only.
Cyanide Skid Detox HAZOP HAZOP Facilitator Facilitated a HAZOP study for the cleaning and detoxification of a cyanide production skid prior to the skids location from a Gold Mine in Tasmania to another site in Queensland for Synergen Met Pty Ltd.
Karbib Lithium Concentrator Project HAZOP HAZOP Facilitator Facilitated a HAZOP study for Lycopodium Minerals Engineering, Lepidico Limited Lepidolite Karbib Concentrator in Namibia, involving milling and flotation of a lithium containing mica ore to produce 56700 dtpa of lithium concentrate suitable for further processing to produce 5000 tpa of battery grade lithium hydroxide and other products
Lepidico Phase 1 Chemical Plant HAZOP HAZOP Facilitator Facilitated a HAZOP study for Lycopodium Minerals Engineering, Lepidico Limited Lepidolite Chemical Facility to be located in Abu Dhabi, UAE. The plant involved novel technology developed by Lepidico Limited to produce 5000 tpa of battery grade Lithium Hydroxide Monohydrate and a number of other by-products.

TICOR Cyanide Project HAZOPs HAZOP team lead process Participated in a six week HAZOP covering the detailed design of a 20,000 tpa solid sodium cyanide plant based on Shawinigan Fluohmic reactor technology to produce a 40% liquid sodium cyanide solution, and PCK Technology to convert the cyanide solution to 99% w/w solid sodium cyanide pillows.
FCCU CO Converter replacement HAZOP HAZOP team Snr process The project involved modification to the Fluid Catalytic Cracking Unit Heat Recovery Steam Generator by replacement of the existing CO convertors.
Ammonia Plant HAZOP HAZOP team owners Principal Process Engineer Participated part time in an 8 week duration HAZOP on the design and construction of a 600 tpd Linde-Casale anhydrous ammonia plant in Munich, incorporating conventional steam methane reforming with PSA hydrogen separation and Linde's air separation process to provide nitrogen to the ammonia synthesis loop.
Phosphoric Acid Plant HAZOP HAZOP team owners Principal Process Engineer Participated in a 3 week duration HAZOP on the design and construction of a 1500 tpd P2O5 single stage hemihydrate phosphoric acid plant in Tokyo using Hydro Fertiliser Technology with detail design and construction by Mitsui Engineering and ABB Clough.
Di-Ammonium Phosphate Plant HAZOP HAZOP team owners Principal Process Engineer Participated in a two week duration HAZOP on the design and construction of a 3200 tpd atmospheric slurry diammonium phosphate granulation plant in Lakeland, Florida with detailed design by Jacobs Engineers and construction by Fluor Daniel.
QNI Yabulu Expansion Cobalt Refinery HAZOP HAZOP team lead process Participated in a HAZOP of a design to expand the capacity of the QNI Cobalt refinery as part of the Yabulu Expansion Project to utilise Nickel Cobalt concentrate from the Ravensthorpe nickel project in WA.
